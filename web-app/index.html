Circuit Conquest

A two-player connection strategy game

Players

▲
Player One Connect top to bottom
■
Player Two Connect left to right

Game

How to Play

Rules

  • Player One (▲) places triangles and must form a connected path from the top edge to the bottom edge.
  • Player Two (■) places squares and must form a connected path from the left edge to the right edge.
  • Players alternate turns, placing one marker per turn.
  • Only horizontal and vertical connections count. Diagonal connections do not form a path.
  • The first player to complete a continuous path between their two target edges wins immediately.
  • If the board fills completely with no winner, the game ends in a draw.
  • Occupied cells cannot be selected.
  • No moves can be made after the game ends.

Keyboard Controls

  • Arrow keys — move between board cells.
  • Enter or Space — place a marker on the focused cell.
  • Tab — move from the board to the Restart button and back.
